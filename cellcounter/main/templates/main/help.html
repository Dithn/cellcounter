Help

CellCountr provides a simple way for cells on a peripheral blood or bone marrow aspirate film to be counted and analysed. CellCountr can help you perform counts with ease. Click here to begin.

Instructions

  • Press the key corresponding to a cell type to add a count.
  • Press 'Backspace' to remove last normal/abnormal cell entered.
  • To flag a cell as abnormal press 'Space+Key'.
  • To see example images of the cells in question press 'Shift+Key'.
  • Once your count is complete, click 'Close & Save Results' to see a tabulated report
  • Download as a .csv if you like using the blue button below the report.
